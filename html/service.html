Our Services

We offer weekly and monthly subscriptions to suit your needs, with options to pause deliveries during emergencies. Whether you're looking for fresh vegetables for a small household or bulk orders for larger families, we’ve got you covered.

Weekly Subscription

Get a fresh delivery of organic vegetables every week. Perfect for busy individuals or families who consume vegetables regularly.

Price: 300 tk per week

Monthly Subscription

Receive a full month’s supply of fresh, organic vegetables delivered at your convenience. Ideal for larger households or for those who prefer less frequent deliveries.

Price: 1000 tk per month

Emergency Pause

If you are unable to receive your delivery due to travel or other emergencies, you can pause the delivery for up to two weeks.

Price: Free (for active subscribers)

Why Choose FreshGreens?

We believe in providing the freshest produce at affordable prices. Our subscription services are flexible, and our organic vegetables are sourced directly from farms, ensuring the highest quality.
